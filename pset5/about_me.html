about me

I was born and raised in Andover, Massachusetts where I attended Andover High School, graduating in 2008. I majored in Chemistry at Tufts University, graduating in 2012. I began my graduate studies at Columbia University under the supervision of Prof. Scott A. Snyder, but in 2013 I transferred to the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center where I conducted research under the supervision of Prof. Samuel J. Danishefsky. After completing my PhD in 2017, I began my postdoc with Prof. Eric N. Jacobsen at Harvard University.
